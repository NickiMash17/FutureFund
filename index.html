Frequently Asked Questions

Our calculator uses your current age, income, desired retirement age, and other factors to estimate how much money you'll have at retirement. It accounts for inflation and compound interest to give you the most accurate projection possible.

Financial experts typically recommend saving 15-20% of your income for retirement. However, the exact amount depends on your current age, desired retirement lifestyle, and when you plan to retire. Our calculator can help you determine a personalized target.

The best time to start saving for retirement is as early as possible. Thanks to compound interest, even small amounts saved in your 20s and 30s can grow significantly by retirement age. However, it's never too late to start - our calculator can help you create a plan regardless of your current age.
